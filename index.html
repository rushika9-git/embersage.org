About Us

EmberSage is a nonprofit community initiative dedicated to helping teenagers and families find balance, creativity and connection through arts, wellness and movement. We provide safe spaces where young people can explore, express and recharge.

Our Events

Every Saturday starting January 2 2026, from 5–6 PM, we host a rotating set of events designed to relax, inspire and connect teens.

Art Night

An evening of painting, sketching and creative expression. No experience needed—just come and play.

Mindful Yoga Session

A gentle yoga and meditation class to help you stretch, breathe and unwind from the week.

Walk Together

A group walk in nature, with time to talk, reflect and enjoy the outdoors in good company.

Dance/Zumba Workout

A fun, high‑energy dance workout session that gets everyone moving and smiling.
